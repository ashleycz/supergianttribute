Overview

Supergiant Games is a development company known for their unique visuals, music and gameplay. Started in 2009 by programmers Amir Rao and Gavin Simon with musician Darren Korb and artist Jen Zee, their creativity and passion have so far culminated in two critically acclaimed games with a third on the way.

"Bastion is the first title from Supergiant Games, an original action role-playing game set in a lush imaginative world, in which players must create and fight for civilization's last refuge as a mysterious narrator marks their every move." Bastion is a 2D isometric game in which players play as "the Kid", a young boy struggling to survive after "the Calamity". The game is widely praised for it's narration, with the events of the game told to the player like an old folk tale. Bastion has sold over 3 million copies by January 2015 since it's release in July 2011 and has won awards from the Game Developers Conference, the Academy of Interactive Arts & Sciences, and many others.

Transistor

Transistor is a sci-fi themed action RPG that invites you to wield an extraordinary weapon of unknown origin as you fight through a stunning futuristic city.

Pyre

Pyre is a party-based RPG in which you lead a band of exiles to freedom through an ancient competition spread across a vast, mystical purgatory.
